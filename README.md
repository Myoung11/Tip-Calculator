# Tip-Calculator
The program takes users meal total, asks them the percent they want to tip, then it will tell them how much to tip. 


Example Input/Output: 
********************************************                                                               
 Enter Meal Total: .......... $67.89                                                                       
 Percent to tip(15 = 15%): ... 15                                                                          
 - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -                                                                     
 - Gratuity Amount: ........ $10.18                                                                     
 - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -                                                                     
********************************************
